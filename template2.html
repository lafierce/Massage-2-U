Outcall & Chair Massage
for Home, Hotel, and Events

Massage2U is an outcall massage business serving Houston, TX. Our mobile massage therapists provide table massages for your personal sessions. We also provide chair massage for corporate events, trade shows, employee wellness, and private parties. We are based in Montrose but provide service for a large area including The Heights, River Oaks, Bush Intercontinental and Hobby airports. You choose your massage therapist, date and time. We can come to your home, hotel or place of business. You can view the therapists online profile to learn more about them and the massage modalities they are trained in. We have men and women therapists that can suit our male and female clients needs. You can view all therapists up-to-the minute schedules and book appointments online. Secure credit, debit, and PayPal payments can be made online. A PayPal account is not required.

The therapist of your choice may be in a session. Please allow 2 hours advanced notice. If you need a massage on short notice please call and we may be able to accommodate you. 24 hour advanced noticed will be needed for couples massage, tandem massage, corporate events, and private parties.
